## Review Request Process (GBP Setup)

### How to get your Google Review link:

1. Go to business.google.com and sign in
2. Click your TPS Pro LLC listing
3. In the left menu, click "Ask for reviews"
4. Google generates a direct review link — it looks like: `https://g.page/r/[yourID]/review`
5. Copy this link and save it somewhere permanent (Notes, your phone contacts, email signature)
6. Shorten it using bitly.com for easier texting: e.g., `bit.ly/TPSProReview`

### How to ask for reviews:

**Best time to ask:** Within 2–4 hours of completing a job, while the client is satisfied and it's fresh.

**Hierarchy of who to ask first:**
1. Any client who says "great job," "looks amazing," or similar verbal praise — ask immediately
2. Long-term recurring clients who have never left a review
3. Clients after their first month of service (once they've had time to form an opinion)
4. Property managers after a successful turnover season

**What NOT to do:**
- Don't ask in bulk (Google flags sudden surges)
- Don't offer incentives for reviews (against Google policy)
- Don't ask unhappy clients (obvious, but worth saying)
- Don't ask family/friends who weren't real clients
