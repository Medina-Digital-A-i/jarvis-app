## How to Get Your Google Review Link (Do This First)

Before sending any review requests, get your direct Google review link:

1. Go to **business.google.com** → sign in
2. Click your TPS Pro LLC listing
3. In the dashboard, look for **"Get more reviews"** or **"Ask for reviews"**
4. Google shows a shareable link — looks like: `https://g.page/r/[yourcode]/review`
5. Copy it. Save it to your phone notes, your email signature, and anywhere you'll need it.
6. **Shorten it:** Go to bitly.com, create a free account, shorten to something like `bit.ly/TPSProReview` — this is what you'll paste into texts

**Your review link should go in every template below where you see `[YOUR GOOGLE REVIEW LINK]`**

## Responding to Reviews (Miguel's Job)

### For every 5-star review — respond within 24 hours:

**Template:**

> Thank you, [Name]! We appreciate the kind words. It was a pleasure working with you at [property/building]. The TPS Pro team works hard to deliver consistent, quality service — feedback like this keeps us motivated. We look forward to continuing to serve you. — Miguel, TPS Pro LLC

**Tip:** Vary the wording — Google notices identical responses and it looks automated.

---

### For any negative review (1–3 stars) — respond within 4 hours:

**Template:**

> [Name], thank you for the feedback — I'm sorry we didn't meet your expectations. I'd like to hear more about what happened so I can make it right. Please call me directly at (518) 948-7156 or email info@totalpropertysolution.net. — Miguel, TPS Pro LLC

**Critical rule:** Never argue, never get defensive. Take it offline immediately. A professional, calm response to a negative review impresses future clients more than the negative review hurts you.

### Who Sends It

**In the short term (just Miguel):** Send manually from your personal Gmail or business email. Keep it personal. The personal touch closes more reviews than automated sequences.

**When you're ready to automate:** Use a tool like:
- **Jobber** (field service management — has built-in review request automation)
- **NiceJob** (review generation platform — integrates with Google)
- **Mailchimp** (for email sequences only — no text)
- **HubSpot CRM free tier** (contact tracking + email sequences)

For now, manual is fine and probably better — property managers and facility directors know the difference between a mass email and a personal one.

---

### How to Track

**Minimum tracking (free):**

1. Create a simple Google Sheet titled "Review Tracker"
2. Columns: Client Name | Job Date | Email 1 Sent | Text Sent | Email 2 Sent | Email 3 Sent | Review Left (Y/N) | Stars | Notes
3. Check your GBP listing weekly to see new reviews
4. Log every outreach in the sheet

**Goal metrics:**
- Review request response rate: aim for 20–30% (industry average for service businesses is 10–20%)
- Target: 2–3 new Google reviews per month minimum
- Target: 25+ total Google reviews within 12 months (this is when your local pack ranking gets strong)
- Minimum star rating: maintain 4.5+ stars
